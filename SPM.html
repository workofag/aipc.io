INFRARED Oven for artificial limbs


The system is designed for softening of Poly propylene sheets and tubes by Infrared Heating , to enable manufacturing of Artificial limbs. In conventional convection ovens an object is heated by absorbing ambient heat in a closed compartment. In contrast, the heat in an infrared oven is generated in the form of electromagnetic waves absorbed by the material directly. This has many advantages: Higher speed, greater productivity and a significantly greater energy efficiency are the result. Infrared ovens do not need to be pre-heated – the radiant heat is fully available in an instant. This significantly reduces energy consumption in the orthopaedic workshop. infrared oven heats plastics in a very short time. A 4 mm thick polypropylene sheet can be heated in just 4 to 6 minutes. The system consists of SS 304 Fabricated structure, in lines with requirements of medical industry. System is designed in a way, so that maintenance can be done easily. There is a temperature control arrangement to provide customer flexibility to set temperature. Heating chamber has insulated doors to prevent heat loss.

Wet Bench


We are manufacturer of wet bench. We can provide custom design to meet specific customer requirements. Typically, a wet bench is a fully automated process tool used to carry out wet cleaning and etching operations in semiconductor manufacturing or other high technology products. They can be designed for acid or solvent processing. Benches commonly include several tanks each containing either cleaning or etching solutions and deionized water in a rinsing tank. A drying module is often included in a wet bench.
