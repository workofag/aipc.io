1. BELT CONVEYOR INFRARED OVEN/ IR Oven


Infrared heating is highly efficient because the process heats only the product, not the surrounding air. The heat generated by Infrared waves is faster and more efficient than traditional heating method. Heated times for infrared emitters is minimal, reducing energy costs. The equipment can be turned on minutes before production. Infrared ovens require less maintenance. The choice of short, medium, or long wavelengths for any custom oven or heating system will be dependent on your chosen application process. We provide an exclusive range of Heating conveyorized Ovens. We design, engineer, and manufacture heat processing equipment based on our customer's specific application and process requirements. For designing a Conveyorized Oven there are many factors to consider such as: type of application, operating temperatures, oven type, oven size, heat source, available power, oven features and oven options. Our experienced engineers will guide you through this critical process. All these systems can be designed with electric heater or combination of electric and IR heaters. Some application of Infrared/ Electric Conveyorized Oven:

Adhesive Bonding

Adhesives are used in many industries, such as automotive, electronics, rubber, paper, shoes and others. Some adhesives can be cured at room temperature, while some are optimally cured by heat. A batch oven or conveyor oven can be used for adhesive curing based on the customer’s production requirements. These adhesive curing ovens provide excellent temperature uniformity, so your products and adhesives receive the optimal cure.


Drying

Drying ovens are used across multiple industries for wide range of applications. Primary purpose of this application is to evaporate excess Water/ solvent from the materials like glass, plastic, ink, metal parts, etc. Each unit is custom built according to customer’s requirement. Use of our industry standard infrared heating elements, together with quality programmable controllers, ensures improved reliability, processing time, increased productivity, low maintenance and running costs.


Paint Curing Ovens

Paint Curing oven or paint baking oven removes the moisture. These ovens can be manufactured according to the requirements of customers and requires less floor space.


Rubber Curing Oven

There is wide range of curing application found in a number of industries. A batch Oven or Conveyorized oven can be used for curing process based on the customer’s production requirements. We manufacture industrial curing ovens in both batch and conveyor configuration. These ovens are used to cure powder coating, epoxy resin, thermoplastics, adhesives, coating, rubber, polymer, carbon fibre, plastic composites and many more. These curing ovens provide excellent temperature uniformity, so your parts receive the optimal cure.


PCB De-Soldering

The oven contains multiple zones, which can be individually controlled for temperature. Generally there are several heating zones followed by one or more cooling zones. The PCB moves through the oven on a conveyor belt, and is therefore subjected to a controlled time-temperature profile. The heat source is normally from infrared heaters, which transfers the heat by means of radiation.


Ink Drying

For digital printing, the ink must dry before the paper reaches the folder. Water-based inks can be dried with either hot air or infrared energy. Infrared Dryers improve quality and maintain the press speed consistent with high speed printing. We offer a full range of electric dryers for both web and sheet applications.




Preheating

We design and built a wide range of preheat oven for industries. Industrial preheating ovens are used for heating product prior to forming, coating, fitting, or welding. Products are simply placed in industrial preheating oven for a designated time frame as they are being prepared for next step in the production process. Every custom built preheat oven is always designed and manufactured around the customer own process.




Thermoforming

Thermoforming ovens heat sheets of plastic to a high temperature so they can be stretched and molded into a shape. Thermoformed plastics are commonly used for the automotive, consumer products, packaging, and industrial applications. We manufactures thermoforming ovens in both batch and conveyor configurations. One example below is the soften the plastic component of rear view mirror, to enable smooth fitting of rear view mirrors in mould
