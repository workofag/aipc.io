Conveyor Systems


Conveyors are the most simple, important and effective way of automation of material handling systems in any industry. It enables Industries to increase speed and agility in their processes and hence, improve output. Conveyor systems are typically used to move goods from one location to another, to enable agility in 2 processes.

Conveyor Systems are typically used - in Manufacturing, Automobile, Food Processing, Chemical, Packaging, Pharmaceuticals, Agriculture, Plastics, Electronics & Electrical, Aerospace and many other industries - to speed up the production process and also to optimise cost in manual effort. Conveyors also streamline the assembly process. Therefore, most of the industries are investing in conveyors. Specialized Belt Conveyor


For conveying materials which are of special type, there is still a need for special belts. These types of conveyors have special engineering requirements. Our team of highly skilled professionals have a lot of experience to build these types of specialized conveyors.

There are primarily 3 types of Specialized Belt conveyors

Modular Belt Conveyor

Modular Plastic Belt Conveyors utilise hinged, interlocking, plastic segments to form the belt. This allows flexibility of the movement of the conveyor. These conveyors are used for specialized material movement needing a hard belt. Modular belt conveyor is ideal for applications where a narrow conveyor belt is required. Apart from their rugged nature, these types of conveyors have better serviceability, allowing for easy replacement of the damaged part of the belt without removing whole belt, thereby reducing the amount of down time and belt replacement costs.

Slat Conveyor

Slat conveyors are conveyors which have steel slats. Slats can be of different thickness depending upon the material of weight that is conveyed on it. Slats are placed very close to each other so that the material can move easily on the conveyor. Even fixtures can be installed on slats to carry different types of jobs on them.
Focused onto equipment design keeping ergonomics in consideration, we pay importance to principles pertaining to efficiency, weight, standardization, operational safety, speed and capacity so that we can come up with Slat Conveyors which fulfill application and requirement in a satisfactory way.

Applicable Industries: Automobile, Electric Manufacturing, Manufacturing Line, Blade Hardening

Applicable Process: Special Material movement

Roller Conveyor


Roller conveyors are available in two models: one is motorized (or powered roller) conveyor and other is, Gravity force roller conveyor.

Gravity / Manual Roller Conveyor

Manual Roller Conveyor is one of the simple conveying equipment that works on the principle of force of gravity for hauling and moving the packages and boxes. It requires little friction and is integrated with the Channel to affect the movement of material from one place to another. The slope of the conveyor can be adjusted through the swivel brackets at the end. The end stopper prevents the job from falling. Rollers can be used from mild steel, stainless steel or even PVC as per suitability. The roller conveyor is an ideal device for gentle handling of cartons. Our conveyors are custom fabricated in different sizes and length of rollers in accordance with weights and loads the client wants to move.

Applicable Industries: Any

Applicable Process: Any
